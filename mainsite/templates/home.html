    Home

    Hello and welcome. I created this website as part of my dissertation on international student mobility. In my dissertation, I look at where international students enroll, and whether this has anything to do with where they come from and where they go to. In order to make my conclusions more accessible, I have adapted the chapters so that they are easier and more fun to read. Have a look at the table below to see which question each chapter answers!

    You can download the complete dissertation here. All the code used to create this website can be found here.
